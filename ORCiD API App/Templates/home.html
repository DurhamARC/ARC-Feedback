Durham University Research Finder

Welcome to Durham University Research Finder.

The purpose of this app is to help speed up the process of keeping the ARC team up to date with your current research.

Follow the link to the relevant page, either publications or funding.

From there you will be asked to enter your ORCiD ID, the app will then return items from your record to you in the form of a checkbox list.

Simply check all the work or funding that currently applies and click the submit button.

This data is then stored for the ARC team to keep track of.
